This is just a compilation of personal back office tools I use at work. 
I always open multiple third-party websites just to get to them. 
By creating this webpage: It eliminates the multiple tabs open; Risk on data breach and security; Reduces trouble on navigating between tabs.
Alarm Clock - Let's you choose the time to wake up. Added the Loop function, Cancel alarm and linkbox to add audio files from Dropbox and Discord.
US Time Zone in all of it's states. 
Floating webcam - to convert your regular webcam to spycam at work. You can use it to watch your back if someone is coming. It also has PIP feature so you can do other tasks while the camera is minimized and floating on your screen. Functions like kill switch and copy image were added. 
Calculator with history and optional on-screen numpad. Split function was added. It's like Division but the remainder is distributed evenly making the quotient whole numbers. Useful if you want to distribute objects equally whitout cutting them. 
Quick notes. Just regular notes that can save any text locally.
